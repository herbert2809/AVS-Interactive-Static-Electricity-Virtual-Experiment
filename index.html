How it works

Experiment

1. Click "Wipe Cloth" repeatedly to charge the ruler.
2. Move the ruler over the paper bits.
3. Watch them jump!

Science

Friction transfers electrons between the cloth and the ruler, creating an electric charge.

This charge creates an invisible field that attracts neutral objects like paper. If the force is stronger than gravity, the paper lifts up!
